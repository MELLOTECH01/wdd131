Real Estate and Car Sales

Site Name: Prime Deals Hub

This name reflects the dual-purpose nature of the website—offering prime real estate and car deals in one convenient location. It's memorable, relevant, and professional.

Site Purpose

The website is designed to showcase listings of real estate properties and cars available for sale. It will serve as a platform where potential buyers can browse, compare, and contact sellers easily. The site will include detailed descriptions, images, prices, and contact forms for inquiries.

Scenarios

  • “I’m looking for an affordable 3-bedroom home in Lagos. Can I search based on location and budget?”
  • “I need a reliable used car for under ₦5 million. What are the available options?”

Color Schema

  • Navy Blue (#002B5B): Used for headers and navigation bar to convey trust and professionalism.
  • Gold (#FFD700): Used for call-to-action buttons and highlights to attract attention.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings to give a modern, clean look.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text to ensure readability and elegance.
